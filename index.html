About Me

Jann Conrad Bonifacio, 21 years old, a Broadcast Communication Student and freelance photojournalist based in Manila, Philippines. He is a Chief Photographer in The Communicator - The Official Student Publication of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines under the College of Communication.

He is also a photo contributor to PonD News Asia that his works have been published in the said news outlet. Conrad has also been a former Intern of News5 Digital of TV5 Network Inc and currently an Intern at ABS-CBN News under the supervision of Patrol ng Pilipino.

Award

"For nearly two years, I've been honing my skills as a photographer, capturing a wide range of subjects with an eye for detail and creativity. My focus is on creating visually compelling images that tell a story, whether it's portrait, landscape, or product photography. I continuously experiment with different techniques and styles to bring out the best in every shot, ensuring that the final product is not just a photograph, but a work of art."

2nd Most Outstanding Photojournalist for University of the Assumption - 4th Luzon-wide Outstanding Campus Press Awards (AOCPA), March 1, 2024.

3rd Place for Photo Essay Category, Philippine Journalism Research Conference (PJRC) 2024, June 7, 2024.
